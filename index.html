Bomb Lab
Project

For my computer organization class, one of my projects was Bomb Lab. Bomb Lab consists of six unique phases written in assembly language testing one's understanding of registers and memory allocation through a variety of computer science topics such as recursion, switch cases, loops, and linked lists. The goal is to defuse the bomb one phase at a time, however if an attempt to defuse a phase is invalid, the bomb explodes.

November 2023

Attack Lab
Project

For my computer organization class, one of my projects was Attack Lab. Attack Lab tests one's understanding of buffer overflow errors, registers, exploiting weaknesses in operating systems and network servers, and learning how to write programs that are more secure in addition to some of the features provided by compilers and operating systems to make programs less vulnerable. Unlike the Bomb Lab, an invalid attempt is known as a misfire or segmentation fault. I created a unique code injection (CI) attack and a unique return-oriented programming (ROP) attack for each of the five phases throughout the lab.
